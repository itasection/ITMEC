Alumni Talk on "BUILDING A SUCCESSFUL CAREER IN SOFTWARE ENGINEERING"

Date: August 30, 2025

Resource Person: Ms. Karishma Misra. V (DIGITAL MARKETING EXECUTIVE, Seyfert Infotech Pondicherry)

Venue: Tech Block

Description:
The Department of Information Technology recently hosted an insightful Alumni Talk featuring one of our senior alumni, who shared her experiences in the field of Digital Marketing. During the session, she discussed her role in her company and explained how digital marketing contributes to brand growth and business success. She highlighted important aspects such as strategic planning, content creation, social media management, SEO, and analytics, giving students a clear picture of the skills and knowledge required in this dynamic field. Through her engaging talk, students learned practical tips on creating effective campaigns, using industry tools, and staying updated with emerging trends. The session concluded with valuable guidance on continuous learning, gaining hands-on experience, and exploring career opportunities in digital marketing, leaving the students inspired and motivated to pursue this path.
