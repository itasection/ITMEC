Celesta'25 - National Level Technical Symposium

Date: September 13, 2025

Venue: IT Lab 1 & 2, Tech Block, Mailam Engineering College

Prizes: Exciting Cash Awards, Certificates & Goodies

Description:
Celesta'25 is a prestigious national-level symposium hosted by the Department of Information Technology, featuring an electrifying lineup of events like coding marathons, paper presentations, gaming battles, project expos, and technical debates. It offers a platform for innovation, skill display, and networking with IT aspirants across the country.

Participants will have the opportunity to interact with industry professionals, attend expert talks, and showcase their talents in front of esteemed judges. Be a part of the celebration of technology and intellect!
