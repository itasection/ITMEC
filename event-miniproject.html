Mini Project Expo

Date: September 5, 2025

Venue: Lab 2, IT Block

Prizes: Best Project Award, Certificates

Description:
The Mini Project Expo provides a platform for final year students to present their technical creativity and practical knowledge. The event will showcase a wide range of innovative projects addressing real-world challenges.

Judges from industry and academia will evaluate the projects based on innovation, implementation, and societal impact. All are welcome to witness the next generation of tech leaders in action!
